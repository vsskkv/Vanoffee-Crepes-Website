The Beginning

It all started in a tiny kitchen in 2020, during the most unexpected of times. Sarah, our founder, found herself with an abundance of time and a craving for the perfect crepe - the kind she'd tasted during her travels through France.

What began as a way to bring joy to her family quickly became something bigger. Friends started asking for her "magical crepes," and neighbors would knock on her door, drawn by the irresistible aroma wafting through the street.

Our Journey

2020

The Kitchen Experiment

Sarah's first perfect crepe recipe was born in her home kitchen, inspired by memories of Parisian street vendors.

2021

First Market Stall

With a portable crepe maker and a dream, Vanoffee Crepes made its debut at the local farmers' market.

2022

Growing Community

Word spread like butter on a hot pan. We became a regular fixture at events, festivals, and private parties.

2023

Expanding Horizons

Today, we're serving up happiness at weddings, corporate events, and bringing joy to communities across the region.
