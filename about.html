Our Story

A Sweet Journey From Dream to Reality

Discover how a simple passion for perfect crepes turned into a beloved local sensation

The Beginning

It all started in a tiny kitchen in 2020, during the most unexpected of times. Sarah, our founder, found herself with an abundance of time and a craving for the perfect crepe - the kind she'd tasted during her travels through France.

What began as a way to bring joy to her family quickly became something bigger. Friends started asking for her "magical crepes," and neighbors would knock on her door, drawn by the irresistible aroma wafting through the street.
